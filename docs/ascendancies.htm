Ascendancies are powerful specializations that shape how you play, granting unique skills and bonuses.
Ascendancies are granted through a special charm called the Ascendancy Stone that can only be obtained after completing several special encounters.
Ascendancy stones can be further upgraded (meaning you can further ascend) by completing specific challenges, some of which are tied to very hard endgame content.
There are 5 ascendancy tiers you can attain, each challenge being harder than the previous one, but the bonuses being stronger as well.
Needless to say, each tier can only be achieved once. However, you can achieve them in any order.
Due to an engine limitation, the special effect from tier 4 will not apply if you haven't ascended to tier 3 first. Once you ascend to tier 3, tier 4 will automatically take effect.

Tier 1 Challenge

In order to get an Ascendancy Stone, you need to kill 5 special superuniques scattered around Sanctuary
and transmute the elemental orbs each of them drop to obtain the Orb of Creation.
The orb can then be transmuted with specific colored gems to unlock the ascendancy of your choice.

Tier 2 Challenge

The second tier is achieved by killing Baal on hell difficulty and transmuting the ascendancy stone with itself.

Tier 3 Challenge

The third tier is achieved by killing all of Uber Tristram bosses within one minute of each other and transmuting the ascendancy stone with a Maple Leaf.
Input Output
Ascendancy Stone
Maple Leaf 		Ascendancy Stone
Upgraded to Tier 3

Tier 4 Challenge

The fourth tier is achieved by killing any of the Invincible Prime Evils in Eye of the Storm Tier 5 Map and transmuting the ascendancy stone with 2 Maple Leaves.
Input Output
Ascendancy Stone
2 Maple Leaves 		Ascendancy Stone
Upgraded to Tier 4

Tier 5 Challenge

The fifth tier is the most difficult tier to achieve and will test your build to the limit. You have to kill the Lich King in Icecrown Citadel Tier 5 Map on players 8 difficulty and transmute the ascendancy stone with 3 Maple Leaves.
Input Output
Ascendancy Stone
3 Maple Leaves 		Ascendancy Stone
Upgraded to Tier 5
